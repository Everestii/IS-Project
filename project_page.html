Our Projects

Celebrating creativity, connection, and community.

What We Do

At TogetherN'Art, we run dynamic projects such as collaborative art workshops, community murals, and art therapy sessions. Each project is designed to unite individuals across generations, fostering mutual understanding and creativity.

Future Plans

  • Launching a monthly intergenerational painting event.
  • Expanding our community mural program to new cities.
  • Introducing digital art workshops to reach remote participants.
